About me

Hi, I'm Astrid. I'm a Software Engineer and I want to help people grow confidence in their tech skills.

I would like to inspire people to believe in themselves and show them that they can from having zero tech experience to building an automated watering system in several hours.

I want people to understand that they are capable to understand technology, and challenge common stereotypes of a career in engineering only being attenable for certain types of people.

I'm a self-taught Software Engineer, so I understand how hard it can be and hope I'll be able to help people with their own journeys.
