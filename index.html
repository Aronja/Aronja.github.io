A bit of background...

Today is all about building an automated watering system using a Raspberry pi computer, a relay and a submersed pump. You can also easily build this at home if you follow these instructions.

Don't worry no prior computing or tech skills are required and everyone is welcome to participate. Ideally work in teams and help each other, following the guide step by step.

This is what the finished MVP looks like. :) I built while I had Covid and was at home self-isolating.



Go big or go home, this watering system can also be used for multiple plants and has helped me keep mine alive.
